The Websites that I like

  • Webtoons- Even though it is busy, it makes sense. Its structure is sound and has this vibrant, cartoon theme. I like the moving transitions of the weekly highlights.
  • Amazon- The home page is long but organized and tailored to my familys' wants base on past history. Different themes are divided neatly.
  • Indeed- It is very simplistic and sets the mood for a user to get straight to business. I like the view of job options on one column and the chosen job position on the other.

The Websites that I do not like

  • Manganelo- This homepage is not welcoming and it is overwhelming. It is not until you scroll to the end of the page until you can pick specific genres.
  • Youtube- It is a cosmic trap that pulls you into the world of videos longer than you want to. The home page is compacted with videos of recommendations, suggestions and subscriptions with minimum division.
  • Facebook- The layout smashes three columns into a single page of chaos. Two station columns with a moving timeline in between them seems too much for the main navigation page.
